THE MISSION

Welcome to the AI+! We are a group of passionate researchers, hackers, entrepreneurs and enthusiasts envisioning the future powered by (morally good) AI. Let's watch AI disrupt the world, vertical by vertical. AI+ will start with a series of AI + X events. To tease a bit, "X" can be transportation, healthcare, education, law enforcement, finance, etc. We'll start with tech talks and panel discussions, but we are actively exploring creative ways to catalyze and share ideas.
