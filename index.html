AI+
CMU AI+ is an organization to bring together researchers, entrepreneurs and domain experts at Carnegie Mellon University with a shared vision of "AI is the new electricity".

THE MISSION

AI+ is a "tribe" of researchers, hackers, entrepreneurs and enthusiasts envisioning the future powered by morally good AI. Sometimes, we discover those hidden gems in different verticals before their first debut to the world. We want to make AI+ an intellectual hub that connects AI research and the industry, as well as a place that nurtures new ideas. This AI+ club at CMU is a partner of the Stanford AI+ club.
